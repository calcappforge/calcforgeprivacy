Privacy Policy

Trade Suite is developed and operated by CalcForge.

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the Trade Suite mobile application.

Information Collection

Trade Suite does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

Advertising

The app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information such as advertising ID, IP address, and interaction data in order to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can learn more about Google’s data practices here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

Trade Suite offers in-app purchases processed by Google Play Billing. All payment information is handled by Google. CalcForge does not receive or store your payment details.

Data Usage

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. Any data processed by third-party services (such as Google AdMob or Google Play) is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Children’s Information

Trade Suite is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
